About Jackie.

He discovered his passion for developing more articulate software experiences, helping others, and having genuine interactions while working on the Eagle Team (Automotive) in the CRM and Product Management divisions at Epicor Software Corp. Jackie Wilson is excited to apply their skills and knowledge to the professional arena.

Known for being creative, organized, and consistent, Jackie Wilson is an asset to any team.
